## Information

Multiverse Computing is a company focused on efficient and sovereign AI, with public messaging around quantum-inspired techniques, model compression, and production-ready AI deployment. Its product CompactifAI is positioned as a way to compress large language models so they require less memory, compute, and energy while preserving useful performance.

The company links model compression to several strategic benefits: lower inference costs, reduced energy use, faster deployment, edge or on-prem operation, data sovereignty, and more control over AI infrastructure. This is relevant because many organizations want AI capabilities without sending all data to external cloud APIs or paying indefinitely for hosted inference.

CompactifAI is described as a post-training compression approach. Public materials say it can reduce model size substantially without retraining from scratch. TechRadar coverage describes the method as quantum-inspired tensor-network compression that restructures transformer weight matrices rather than merely deleting weights. The key claim is not just "smaller model," but a more efficient representation that keeps useful behavior.

Multiverse announced a USD 215M raise in 2025 to scale its LLM compression technology. The company is based in Spain and often associated with San Sebastian. Its positioning sits at the intersection of AI infrastructure, compression, quantum-inspired computing, sovereign AI, and enterprise deployment.
